Pet the Finn

Step in the shoes of Jessicah.

Another day, another game of Siege. Defend yourself from the endless array of enemies that pop up left and right. Don't let your health bar get to 0!

But what's that?! Finn demands pets!

And o no! Someone just subbed! Let's hope the alert sound plays for everybody.

Features

  • In good ol' fashioned Siege manner, the hitboxes on this are broken as well and I refuse to fix them.
  • When Finn spawns, it lowers your sensitivity cause he's build like a tater tot and is sitting on your lap.
  • The more you play, the more demanding Finn gets.
  • After every 4 enemies you kill, you regenerate some health.
